ABOUT US

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering , Kanjirapally, is the first and the only new generation college in Kerala to secure the prestigious NBA accreditation for prime departments. The main features of the College comprise world-class infrastructure, top-flight faculty, high pass percentage, excellent placement record and unique student projects. Amal Jyothi College of Engineering i is a mega complex of nearly 7 lakh sq.ft. overlooking the busy Kanjirapally-Erumeli stretch of the Kottayam-Sabarimala State highway. The two campus hostels, taken care of by dedicated Catholic priests and sisters, have a capacity of 1350 inmates. The campus has 12 Mbps internet connectivity with WiFi. Amal Jyothi College of Engineering is the only engineering college in the state to have a Catholic priest as Principal who secured first rank in both B Tech (Calicut University) and M Tech (IIT Kharagpur) The Name "AMAL JYOTHI" The word "Amal"means immaculate and inviolate, and "Jyothi" means light....
